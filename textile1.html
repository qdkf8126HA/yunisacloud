DISORDER

Disorder

For this interesting fabric experiment, I tried a variety of different methods to make a total of 24 pieces of cloths. I pieced all of them together and made a compete canvas. The process of creation was very free and causal. Whenever I had some inspiration, I immediately transformed it and reflected it in my work. It was an experience of complete self-release, without caring for the size of the work, the proportion of dyes or the heat of the firelock. Each of the pieces of cloth is representative of spontaneous inspiration.

《杂乱无章》：这是一次有趣的面料实验，我尝试了很多不同方法总共制作了24块布料，最终将他们拼接成一块完整的画布。创作过程非常随性洒脱，稍有灵感就把它立刻转化成现实，这是一次释放自我的创作体验。不需要精细的计算尺寸，调制染料的混合比列，把握火枪的火候。每一块布料的呈现都是来自杂乱无章的尝试。
